When you create an Account, we will collect personal information from you, such as your name and email address. One feature of the Services is the ability to create shortened uniform resource locators (URLs) of websites (“utrim.me Links”). Some features of the Services allow you to provide content to the Services, such as written descriptions of URLs, comments, images and video. Information Collected Automatically:

utrim.me automatically receives and records information from your web browser when you interact with the Services (including creating or clicking on utrim.me Links), such as your IP address, browser type, your general location and cookie information. We will collect information about your general location (such as your city and state, which is derived from your IP address) when you visit the Site or use the Services, such as by clicking a utrim.me Link and we may use various technologies to determine your location (if you enable this feature), when you access and use the Services through a mobile device. Cookie Policy:

Cookies are pieces of text that may be provided to your computer through your web browser when you access a website. Your browser stores cookies in a manner associated with each website you visit. We use cookies to enable our servers to recognize your web browser and tell us how and when you visit our Site and use the Services through the web.

utrim.me cookies also allow utrim.me to track when you have clicked on a utrim.me Link. Each click of a utrim.me Link is tracked using a unique identifier assigned to you in one or more cookies stored by your web browser and associated with utrim.me. We may associate the unique identifier in our cookies with the other information we automatically collect when you use the Services, as described above, including your IP address, utrim.me Links you click, utrim.me Links you create, and information with your Account if you have one.

Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. If you disable cookies, you will not be able use most features of the Services. utrim.me cookies are set to automatically expire after 6 months from when they are set.

You may also ask utrim.me not to place cookies on your web browser. When cookies are disabled in this manner, clicks on utrim.me Links are not tied back to or associated with your web browser. However, we will still automatically collect the IP addresses of computers or mobile devices that click on or create utrim.me Links.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by utrim.me only and does not cover the use of cookies by any Third Party Services. utrim.me does not control when or how third parties place cookies on your computer, such as when you visit the website from a utrim.me Link.
